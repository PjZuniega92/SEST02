Favorite Food: Sinigang na baboy

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb pork belly (or any pork cut of your choice)
  • 8 cups water
  • 1 medium onion, quartered
  • 2 tomatoes, quartered
  • 1 radish (daikon), sliced
  • 2 cups kangkong (water spinach) or spinach
  • 5-6 pieces okra
  • 1 eggplant, sliced
  • 1-2 green chili peppers (siling haba), optional
  • 1-2 packets of sinigang mix (tamarind seasoning) or fresh tamarind if available
  • Salt or fish sauce to taste

Procedure:

  1. In a pot, add water and bring it to a boil.
  2. Add the pork belly (or any pork cut of your choice) and cook until it becomes tender, skimming off any scum that rises to the top.
  3. Add the onion and tomatoes to the pot and continue to boil until the tomatoes soften.
  4. Once the pork is tender, add the radish and cook for a few more minutes until it softens.
  5. Add the sinigang mix (or fresh tamarind if using) and stir well. Taste and adjust the sourness to your preference.
  6. Season with salt or fish sauce to taste.
  7. Add the eggplant, okra, and green chili peppers, if desired. Cook for a few more minutes until the vegetables are tender.
  8. Finally, add the kangkong (water spinach) or spinach and cook for another minute or until wilted.
  9. Turn off the heat, and your *Sinigang na Baboy* is ready to serve. Enjoy it hot with rice!
